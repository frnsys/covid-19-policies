COVID-19 Policy Response

This is an ongoing archive of US-based public- and private-sector responses to the COVID-19 pandemic. We are collecting examples that demonstrate the things we allegedly can't have, except that we can. Here we track the scope of these changes, who was responsible for making them, where they’re happening, and for how long.
